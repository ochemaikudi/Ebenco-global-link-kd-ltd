It is essential that early recognition of health hazard is established, monitored and controlled in the environment of our operation. EBENCO GLOBAL LINK LIMITED takes a great emphasis on the promotion of good health concept and the protection of its employees and third parties. 1. Prior to the commencement of the project, all workers must be certified Medically fit by the Company’s doctor. 2.We shall provide adequately stocked First aid box on site, and a qualified First Aider/Nurse shall man it. 3.There shall be availability of adequate filtered portable water on site. 4.Provision of Medevac (Medical Evacuation) facilities (Standby vehicle or boat and stretcher) on site in event of emergency 5.We maintain a retainer clinic (REGAL clinic, Warri Delta State). 6.EBENCO GLOBAL LINK LIMITED shall also maintain other retainer-ship clinics, wherever we have projects in land location. 7.For this project EBENCO LIMITED shall maintain its “NO SMOKING” policy as it’s a potential health harzard.
